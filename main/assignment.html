Lenskart: The Visionary Disruptor of Eyewear

Lenskart, founded in 2010 by Peyush Bansal, is one of India's leading eyewear retailers. Over the years, it has grown from a pure online model to an omnichannel business, providing eyewear products both online and through physical stores. 1. A Lens for Every Life: The Omnichannel Experience

Lenskart's Journey: From humble beginnings in 2010 as an online retailer to a seamless 1,200+ store strong omnichannel behemoth, Lenskart delivers style, convenience, and vision. Whether you prefer trying frames on from your couch or in a chic store, Lenskart’s hybrid model gives you the best of both

why it works

  • Online Convenience meets In-Store Experience, allowing customers to explore from anywhere and make confident choices.
  • Their unique Home Try-On service takes customer comfort to another level—imagine trying your top 5 frames without leaving home!

2. Powered by Tech: A Vision Beyond Human Eyes

Ever thought you could virtually try on glasses with a swipe? Lenskart’s AI-powered 3D Try-On feature makes that possible! Its Augmented Reality (AR) experience means you can now pick the perfect frame, custom-tailored to your face, without stepping outside.

How They’re Innovating:

  • Automated Supply Chain—with robotic precision, Lenskart delivers over 10,000 glasses a day, ensuring the highest standards of speed and accuracy.
  • AR & AI tools that scan your face to recommend frames that suit your style and shape.

3. Seeing Beyond Profits: Global Vision, Local Impact

Lenskart is no longer just an Indian success story; it’s a global contender. With a presence in Singapore, the Middle East, and plans to take on the U.S. Lenskart is on a mission to make eyewear accessible, affordable, and stylish for everyone.

International Domination on the Horizon:

  • Its partnership with top investors like SoftBank has supercharged its valuation to over $4 billion.
  • Its global expansion aims to challenge established players like Warby Parker, with cutting-edge tech and stylish frames designed to capture new markets.

4. The Eyes Have It: Fashion Meets Functionality

Lenskart is not just about vision correction—it’s about making eyewear a style statement. With over 5,000 designs, you can find everything from budget-friendly basics to high-end chic, covering all styles and price ranges.

Style-Forward Innovation:

  • Affordable Fashion: No more choosing between style and price—Lenskart ensures you get both.
  • Lens Customization: From UV protection to blue light filters, they’ve got lenses to match your daily demands, whether you’re binge-watching or boardroom hustling.
